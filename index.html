FAQ

Common questions.

Will patients know they're talking to a bot? +
The bot replies in natural Roman Urdu and sounds exactly like a real receptionist. For consultations (Botox, fillers, rhinoplasty), it collects info and promises a doctor callback — never pretends to be the doctor.
Does my clinic need a website? +
No. The bot runs entirely on WhatsApp. We just need your services list and schedule — that's it. No website, no technical setup from your side.
How long does setup take? +
3–5 days maximum. You give us your services, pricing, and doctor schedule. We handle everything else — building, testing, and going live.
What if the bot can't handle something? +
The bot automatically transfers to your staff with full conversation context. No patient gets lost. Your team picks up exactly where the bot left off.
We already use WhatsApp Business — does this replace it? +
It runs on top of WhatsApp. Your existing number and contacts stay the same. Think of it as WhatsApp Business on full autopilot.
Is the first month really free? +
Yes. No credit card, no commitment. We set up the full system, you run it for 30 days — if you don't see the value, you owe nothing. We're confident it pays for itself in the first week.
